Junior Web Developer

I am a dedicated and skilled Junior Web Developer with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from DMI St. Eugene University. With a strong foundation in various web development technologies and a diverse range of professional experiences, I am passionate about creating efficient, scalable, and user-friendly web applications. My career journey includes notable volunteer and internship roles that have honed my technical and analytical skills. As an Assistant ICT Officer with the Ministry of Health, I supported critical ICT operations, contributing to the smooth functioning of health services. At Innovation Africa Zambia (IA), my role as a Data Associate Intern allowed me to delve into data management and analytics, fostering a robust understanding of data-driven decision-making. I further expanded my expertise as an Assistant National Data Warehouse Officer at the Institute for Health Measurements Southern Africa (IHM). In this capacity, I played a key role in data warehouse development and management, executing ETL processes and utilizing SQL Server Integration Services (SSIS) for efficient data integration. My proficiency in data visualization tools such as Superset, PowerBI, and SQL Server Reporting Services (SSRS) has enabled me to present complex data insights in an accessible and impactful manner. I possess comprehensive skills in web development, including PHP, JavaScript, CSS3, HTML5, Bootstrap, JQuery, Ajax, and Laravel. Additionally, I have a foundational understanding and development skillset of the ASP.NET core MVC. My database administration capabilities span across several relational database management systems (RDBMS) including MySQL, PostgreSQL, Microsoft SQL Server, and SQLite, which I proficiently design and develop to meet various organizational needs. All of my technical skills are validated through certifications from LinkedIn Learning, reflecting my commitment to continuous professional development and staying abreast of industry advancements. I am eager to leverage my background and skills to contribute to innovative projects, collaborate with dynamic teams, and drive forward the success of web development and data management initiatives.
